Welcome to MakeUWEC!

MakeUWEC is an interdisciplinary community of students focused on making new and innovative ideas come to life. We hope to inspire students to troubleshoot projects together, collaborate with others, and discover new interests. Our general meetings are held every Friday at 4 pm in the BluGold Makerspace (McIntyre 109). Hope to see you there!
